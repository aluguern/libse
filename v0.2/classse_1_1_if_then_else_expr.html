Detailed Description

Ternary expression of the form "x ? y : z".

Interior vertex in the DAG with three (ordered) children which form a symbolic expression of the form "x ? y : z" where x, y and z are shared expressions such that "x" has a Boolean type and the type of both "y" and "z" are identical.

Warning:
Operands are not type checked
